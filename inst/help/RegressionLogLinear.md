Log-linear Regression
==========================

Log-linear regression models the logarithm of the dependent variable as a linear combination of independent variables. It is useful for handling skewed data and multiplicative relationships.

### Input
---

#### Statistics
- Estimates: Displays estimates of the regression coefficients along with their standard errors, Z-values, and associated p-values.
  - Confidence intervals: Coverage of the confidence intervals in percentages. The default value is 95.
- Vovk-Sellke maximum *p*-ratio: An upper bound on how much more likely a p-value is under the alternative hypothesis than under the null.
